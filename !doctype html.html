THE MERCHANT OF VENICE

BY - SIR WILLIAM SHAKESPEARE

The MoV is a play centering a merchant, Antonio, who borrows some money from an enemy jewish merchant, Shylock, with a deal of half pound of flesh out of Antonio's body.
Due to the shipwrecks that Antonio faced, he came out to be pennyless and was unable to pay shylock back, in time and was hence taken to the court for the fulfillment of the deals.
Later for Antonio's rescue came his best friend bassanio's wife, Portia who was able to get Antonio out of the cruelty of Shylock and the story ended happily.
